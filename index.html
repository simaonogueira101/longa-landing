#Values and Worth

longa., from the latin longaminis, meaning patient, calm. Aiming for the caretaker looking for the next tool to ease their process.


User-focused interfaces, simple UI, simpler UX. Unintrusive notification systems, to the point information.

Focusing on AI and Machine Learning to improve pattern detection, longa. learns with every new user and patient and caretaker. Data stored, maintained and secured on a Blockchain implementation.


Our first product, longa.care - the first palpable iteration over the brand concept, resulting in an iOS App, built specifically for the monitoring of vital signs through the study of the patient’s body temperature & stool and urine - pH, temperature, humidity.
